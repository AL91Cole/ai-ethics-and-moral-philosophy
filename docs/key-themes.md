# Key Themes

## 1. AI is not morally neutral
AI reflects human choices, values, incentives, and structures of power.

## 2. Responsibility stays with humans
When AI causes harm, responsibility should not disappear into the system.

## 3. Ethics can be misused
Organizations sometimes use the language of ethics without making meaningful change.

## 4. Trust must be earned
AI should not be treated as morally trustworthy unless humans remain accountable and users retain meaningful choice.

## 5. AI is a justice issue
AI affects participation, opportunity, dignity, and real human freedom, especially for marginalized groups.
